🐉 Twin Dragon Buffet

Twin Dragon Buffet is a beloved family-owned restaurant with over 50 years of history, proudly serving the community with the finest East Asian cuisine.

Founded on tradition and passion, we specialize in a wide variety of Chinese and Korean dishes, offering guests a rich blend of authentic flavors, fresh ingredients, and comforting hospitality.

🍱 What We Offer:

  • A generous buffet selection with classic Chinese favorites and Korean specialties
  • A warm, welcoming atmosphere suitable for families, friends, and casual dining
  • Consistently praised by our loyal customers for quality and variety

Whether you're craving sweet & sour chicken, spicy kimchi, savory dumplings, or sizzling BBQ — Twin Dragon Buffet delivers a taste of East Asia right in your neighborhood.

Come hungry, leave happy — for over 50 years, that’s been our promise.
